Delete Purrfit Account and Data

Signed-in users can delete synced Purrfit account data directly in the app.

Delete In The App

  1. Open Purrfit.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Make sure you are signed in.
  4. Tap Delete account.
  5. Confirm deletion.

What Is Deleted

This deletes synced Supabase-owned Purrfit data, including profile, onboarding answers, safety-screening rows, avatar settings, workout plans, workout sessions, workout logs, Buddy events, AI adjustment rows, notification settings, ad events, analytics events, subscription mirror rows, and the Supabase Auth user.

Guest Data

Guest data is local to your device. Clear it by uninstalling Purrfit or clearing app storage from your device settings.

What May Remain Elsewhere

Google, app stores, RevenueCat, and payment processors may retain purchase, tax, fraud-prevention, and billing records according to their own policies. Cancel active subscriptions through the app store.
