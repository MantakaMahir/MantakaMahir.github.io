Safety

Purrfit is general fitness guidance, not medical advice. Stop exercising and seek medical help for chest pain, fainting, severe shortness of breath, sharp pain, numbness, swelling, recent surgery concerns, pregnancy/postpartum concerns, or any symptom your clinician told you to treat carefully.
