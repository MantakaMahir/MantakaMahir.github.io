Purrfit Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Purrfit is a gentle fitness app. This policy explains what data the app collects, why it is used, and how to request deletion.

Data We Collect

The app may store your profile name, selected avatar/body settings, onboarding answers, safety-screening answers, workout plan, workout progress, reminders, app settings, local analytics events, Buddy messages, ad events, and subscription status.

How Data Is Used

We use this data to create your workout plan, save progress, show reminders, provide Buddy guidance, measure basic product usage, prevent duplicate workout sync, support account deletion, and manage ads or subscriptions.

Third-Party Services

Purrfit uses Supabase for authentication, database, and Edge Functions. Google Sign-In may be used for account login. Google AdMob may show ads and provide consent controls. RevenueCat may manage subscriptions. Gemini may process Buddy messages to classify workout adjustment requests. These providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

Health and Safety

Purrfit is general fitness guidance, not medical advice. Safety-screening answers and Buddy messages may include sensitive wellness information. Do not enter emergency medical information. Stop exercise and seek medical help for red-flag symptoms.

Local and Synced Data

Guest data is stored locally on your device. Signed-in data may be synced to Supabase so progress can be restored. Some local data can remain on the device until you sign out, delete your account, clear app storage, or uninstall the app.

Ads and Purchases

Free users may see ads. Consent options are shown when required. Plus subscribers should not see ads while subscription status is active. Purchases are handled by app-store billing and RevenueCat, not directly by Purrfit.

Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete synced Purrfit account data from the Profile screen when signed in. You can also follow the instructions on the account deletion page. Some third-party billing, fraud-prevention, tax, or platform records may be retained by Google, RevenueCat, or app stores as required.

Contact

For privacy requests, use the contact options on Purrfit support.
